# 📋 **Simple Instructions for Client - How to Fill the Templates**

## 🎯 **What You Need to Do**

Fill these 5 Excel sheets with your product and material information. **Follow the exact order below.**

---

## 📁 **Step 1: SUPPLIERS (Fill First)**

**What to do**: List all your suppliers (companies that sell you materials)

**Example**:
```
Company Name: "Textile Suppliers Ltd"
Contact Person: "John Doe"
Email: "john@textilesuppliers.com"
Phone: "+91 9876543210"
Status: "active"
```

**Important**: Remember the exact company names - you'll need them in Step 2.

---

## 📁 **Step 2: RAW MATERIALS (Fill Second)**

**What to do**: List all raw materials you use to make products

**Example**:
```
Material Name: "Premium Cotton Yarn"
Category: "Yarn"
Unit: "kg"
Supplier Name: "Textile Suppliers Ltd"  ← Must match Step 1 exactly
Cost Per Unit: 450
Current Stock: 500
```

**Important**: 
- Supplier Name must exactly match the Company Name from Step 1
- Use only the categories listed in the template

---

## 📁 **Step 3: PRODUCTS (Fill Third)**

**What to do**: List all your finished products

**Example**:
```
Product Name: "Premium Carpet 6x9"
Category: "plain paper print"
Color: "Red"
Pattern: "Persian Medallion"
Base Quantity: 10  ← This means you want 10 individual carpets
```

**Important**: Remember the exact product names - you'll need them in Steps 4 and 5.

---

## 📁 **Step 4: PRODUCT RECIPES (Fill Fourth)**

**What to do**: For each product, list what materials are needed to make it

**Example**:
```
Product Name: "Premium Carpet 6x9"  ← Must match Step 3 exactly
Material Name: "Premium Cotton Yarn"  ← Must match Step 2 exactly
Quantity Needed: 2.5
Unit: "kg"
```

**Important**: 
- Product Name must exactly match Step 3
- Material Name must exactly match Step 2
- One product can need multiple materials (multiple rows)

---

## 📁 **Step 5: INDIVIDUAL PRODUCTS (Fill Last)**

**What to do**: Tell the system how many individual pieces to create

**Example**:
```
Product Name: "Premium Carpet 6x9"  ← Must match Step 3 exactly
Quantity to Create: 10
Production Date: "2024-01-15"
Quality Grade: "A"
Storage Location: "Warehouse A"
```

**Important**: 
- Product Name must exactly match Step 3
- System will create 10 individual carpets with unique QR codes

---

## ⚠️ **CRITICAL RULES**

### **1. Exact Name Matching**
- Supplier names must match exactly between Steps 1 and 2
- Product names must match exactly between Steps 3, 4, and 5
- Material names must match exactly between Steps 2 and 4

### **2. Use Only These Categories**

**Product Categories**:
- plain paper print
- degital print
- backing
- felt
- raw material

**Material Categories**:
- Yarn
- Dye
- Chemical
- Fabric
- Thread
- Fiber
- Coating
- Adhesive
- Other

**Colors**:
- Red, Blue, Green, Yellow, Black, White, Brown, Gray, Multi-color, NA

**Patterns**:
- Persian Medallion, Geometric, Floral, Abstract, Traditional, Modern, Digital Art, Standard, RD-1009

**Quality Grades**:
- A+, A, B, C, D

### **3. Required Fields**
- Company Name (Step 1)
- Material Name, Category, Unit, Supplier Name, Cost Per Unit (Step 2)
- Product Name, Category (Step 3)
- Product Name, Material Name, Quantity Needed, Unit (Step 4)
- Product Name, Quantity to Create (Step 5)

---

## 🎯 **Example Workflow**

1. **Step 1**: List "ABC Textiles" as supplier
2. **Step 2**: List "Cotton Yarn" with supplier "ABC Textiles"
3. **Step 3**: List "Red Carpet" as product
4. **Step 4**: List "Red Carpet" needs "Cotton Yarn"
5. **Step 5**: Create 5 individual "Red Carpet" pieces

---

## ✅ **Before Submitting**

1. Check all names match exactly between sheets
2. Make sure all required fields are filled
3. Use only the categories listed above
4. Save as Excel files (.xlsx)

**That's it! The system will handle everything else automatically.** 🎉
